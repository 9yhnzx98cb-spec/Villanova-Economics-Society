What We Do

A society built around three pillars

From speaker events with industry leaders to original undergraduate research, we connect students to the field of economics in ways the curriculum can't.

01

Discourse

Weekly discussions on current events, monetary policy, and the ideas driving today's economic debates — open to all majors and class years.

02

Research

Opportunities to work on faculty-led projects and present original undergraduate research, with pathways toward publication and conference travel.

03

Career

Speaker series, alumni networking, and resume workshops with professionals across consulting, finance, policy, and academia.
